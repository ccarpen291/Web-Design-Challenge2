TEMPERATURE REVIEW

As one approaches the center of the world the temperature increases, then after the center of the world (0 latitude) has been reached the temperature begins to fall again.

This would mean that if you want to vacation in a warm place you want to be closer to the equator, and if you want to vacation in a less warm place you would want to be farther from the equator.
