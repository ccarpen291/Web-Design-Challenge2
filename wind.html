WIND REVIEW

Wind level appears to get more calm as you appraoch the center of the equator (i.e. less wind) and as you go farther away from the equator you get a ltitle more wind

One would argue that if you want a slightly less windy environment that you would go to a place close to the equator. And, if you want a really windy place for vacation go farther away from the equator
